Adam is a law student at the University of Warsaw. 

During his education, he was awarded a number of scholarships, including the UW Rector's scholarship for best students, the Minister of National Education’s scholarship and the Prime Minister of Poland’s scholarship. 

Before joining the SDP team, he gained experience in one of Warsaw's renowned law firms specialising in corporate law, where he expanded his knowledge of various areas of law, including trademark law and protection of personal rights. 

Adam is fluent in English and is actively learning German.
